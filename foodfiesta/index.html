About the Event

Food fiesta is an event to explore varieties of food where food stalls are set up by students themselves and explore their entrepreneurship skills, all under a single campus. Here, we reunite, eat together and develop new skills.

Food Fiesta, is a foodie and friendly event that offers a platform to pitch and sell food products as a one-on-one business individual. As we all know Love for food makes each dish extraordinary. The motto of Food Fiesta is that If someone sincerely loves food and he/she is passionate about transforming it from an average dish to an extraordinary business then this event is a platform to show their talent.
