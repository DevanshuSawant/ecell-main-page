ABOUT THE EVENT

E-CELL SAKEC had organized “Meet The Entrepreneur: An Exceptional One” an interactive session about Entrepreneurship. This session was conducted by Mr.Sanket Kandade, an esteemed entrepreneur currently running the Inkwale association. The event was conducted on 13th October 2022 in the 4th-floor auditorium.

ABOUT THE ENTREPRENEUR

Mr.Sanket Kandade is the Founder of Inkwale association. He successfully completed his chef management from Goa in 2008. In 2011 he founded chefmultimate.com ,Organic tiffin service and handled it successfully till 2018 and got featured in the business outlook. And since 2018 he has been successfully running the Inkwale association.

TIME & ADDRESS

Venue: 4th Floor Seminar Hall , Shah & Anchor Kutchhi Engineering College , Chembur
Time: 3:00 pm
Date: 13th October 2022
