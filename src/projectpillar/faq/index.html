PROJECT PILLAR FAQ

Anyone who is an Indian citizen, a resident of India, at least 18 years old, and in good physical and mental condition is eligible to register for Project Pillar. No one may participate on another person's behalf. The Rules & Regulations must be followed before entering the competition. During the admission process, you will be required to present the required identification documents.

To access the form, log in to "ecellsakec.in" and click the Project Pillar registration banner. Please be aware that the only way we may contact you is if the mobile number you provided is registered with an Indian service provider. This number will be used for all event-related communication. Please make sure the number is available and active at all times.
