About the Event

Food Fiesta is an annual event organized by E-Cell to promote entrepreneurship and provide students with business exposure. For just one day, participants put up a stall. It gives them a glimpse of all the challenges they would encounter as a business. It offers a platform to pitch and sell your food products as a one-on-one business individual.

The individual gains the ability to solve problems and take on obstacles head-on. E-Cell makes sure to stand by them at every step of the way and directs their enterprise toward a profit margin. Additionally, the variety of cuisines and a joyous environment make this event a complete package. The event was an escape from the typical, an appetite of pride where taste met excellence.
