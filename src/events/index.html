Our Initiatives

Women Entrepreneurship

On International Women's Day, E-Cell SAKEC organised a virtual event discussing about women entrepreneurs and how they affect the business sector, to promote entrepreneurship among women, raise awareness of the topic, and teach them more about how to run a firm as well as how to lead and work in a team.

India4Oceans × E-CELL SAKEC

A CSR initiative by E-Cell in collaboration with India4Oceans where the members visited Juhu Beach and cleaned it up in hopes to make India a cleaner and greener place to live in as well as inspire others to keep their surroundings clean. This event was also attended by personalities like Atul Khatri to spread as much awareness as possible.

E-CELL SAKEC strongly believes in industriousness to bring prosperity. Our aim is to help students become vivacious entrepreneurs and lead their ideas into successful business ventures.
