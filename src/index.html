What is E-CELL?

E-CELL SAKEC strongly believes in industriousness to bring prosperity. Our aim is to help students become vivacious entrepreneurs and lead their ideas into successful business ventures. We guide bright minds and shape them for the industry. Organising boot-camps, startup programs and creating awareness on financial aid schemes from the government is our primary objective. E-CELL SAKEC believes in the chant “YES!! IT’S POSSIBLE.” Join us on this endeavour now!!

Objectives

Awareness

To create awareness for entrepreneurial career amongst the staff and students

Motivation

To motivate the students to start businesses that are of high societal relevance and based on innovation.

Mentorship

To guide the staff and students during different phases of starting their business like raising capital for business, its registration, obtaining different clearances from the Government, product development, sales, marketing, etc.

Incubation Centre

To support the startups initiated by the staff and students through the incubation center till they become strong enough to sustain in the market.

Innovate

To create the culture of innovation and creative problem solving in the Institute.
