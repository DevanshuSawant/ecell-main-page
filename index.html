What is E-CELL?

E-CELL SAKEC strongly believes in industriousness to bring prosperity. Our aim is to help students become vivacious entrepreneurs and lead their ideas into successful business ventures. We guide bright minds and shape them for the industry. Organising boot-camps, startup programs and creating awareness on financial aid schemes from the government is our primary objective. E-CELL SAKEC believes in the chant “YES!! IT’S POSSIBLE. Join us on this endeavour now!!

Objectives

Awareness

To raise cognizance amongst the student members regarding the entrepreneurship sector and its practices.

Motivation

To cultivate a mindset that facilitates persistent goal-directed behavioural patterns and vitality in the workplace.

Mentorship

To provide the conditions for affluent and expert advice to be given by a trustworthy advisor.

Incubation Centres

To provide start-ups with technical assistance, infrastructure, and a variety of other resources that may be needed for the start-up to survive and scale.
