What is Project Pillar?

On a quest to look for worthwhile startups that hold the potential to revitalise the business industry by defying stereotypes and pursuing audacious goals. If you have what it takes to transform a good idea into a viable business with aid from an investor, apply for this event and find a pillar that will support your project to the heights you’ve always dreamt of.

Incentives

Networking

Make connections with leading industrialists and venture capitalists who are willing to help you along the way.

Secure funding

Stand a chance to get hold of a potential investor and subsequently secure funding for your startup.

Proof Of Concept

Find out whether your startup is feasible enough to justify the expenses needed to support and develop it.

Customer Acquisition

Create a firm foothold in the market, by developing a larger customer base and consequently increasing revenue.
